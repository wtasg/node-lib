Available Base32 charset variants.
- `rfc4648`: Standard RFC 4648 alphabet (A-Z, 2-7)
- `hex`: Extended hex alphabet (0-9, A-V)
- `crockford`: Douglas Crockford's alphabet (excludes I, L, O, U)
